Frequently Asked Questions

Is Monster Survivors really free?

Yes! The game is completely free to play with no hidden costs, no subscriptions, and no pay-to-win mechanics.

Do I need to download anything?

No downloads required. The game runs entirely in your browser on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.

The game isn't loading. What should I do?

Try refreshing the page, clearing your browser cache, or using a modern browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Edge. If the issue persists, contact us.

Can I play on my phone?

Yes! The game is optimized for mobile devices with touch controls. Works on both iOS and Android browsers.

How do I report a bug?

Email us at contact@monstesurvivors.online with a description of the issue and what device/browser you're using.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2026

At Monster Survivors ("we", "our", or "us"), we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal data you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website monstesurvivors.online.

1. Information We Collect

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, certain information is automatically collected by our analytics and tracking tools:

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on pages, interactions with the game, click patterns, and navigation flow.
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, device type, screen resolution, and language preferences.
  • IP Address: Anonymized IP address used for approximate geographic location (city/country level).
  • Cookies & Similar Technologies: We use cookies and web beacons to provide and improve our service (see Section 4 below).

Information You Provide

If you contact us via email, we collect your email address and any information you include in your message.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To operate, maintain, and improve our website and game experience.
  • To analyze usage trends and understand how visitors interact with our site.
  • To detect and prevent technical issues and fraudulent activity.
  • To respond to your inquiries and support requests.
  • To display relevant advertising (when applicable).

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect and process your data:

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics (Measurement ID: G-B3K0LQ9HZW) to understand how visitors use our site. Google Analytics collects information such as how often users visit, what pages they visit, and what other sites they used prior to coming to our site. Google uses the data collected to track and examine the use of our website and to prepare reports on its activities. You can learn about Google's privacy practices here.

Microsoft Clarity

We use Microsoft Clarity to capture how you interact with our website through heatmaps and session recordings. This helps us improve usability. Clarity collects usage data including mouse movements, clicks, scrolls, and page navigation. You can view Microsoft's privacy policy here.

Online Games IO

Our game content is provided by Online Games IO via an embedded iframe. When you interact with the game, their servers may collect usage data subject to their own privacy policy. We encourage you to review their privacy practices.

Cloudflare

We use Cloudflare as our CDN and security provider. Cloudflare may process certain data for security and performance optimization purposes.

4. Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser. We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function properly (e.g., security, session management).
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site (Google Analytics, Microsoft Clarity).
  • Advertising Cookies: May be set by ad networks to personalize ads (Google AdSense, if applicable).

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. Note that blocking some cookies may affect site functionality.

5. How Google Uses Data

When Google's advertising services are used on this site, Google and its partners may collect and use data for ad personalization and measurement. You can learn more about how Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request that we correct inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your data.
  • Opt-out: Opt out of cookies and tracking through your browser settings.
  • GDPR Rights (EEA users): Right to data portability, restriction of processing, and to withdraw consent at any time.
  • CCPA Rights (California users): Right to know what personal information is collected, and to opt out of the sale of personal information.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us.

7. Data Retention

We retain analytics data for a period of up to 26 months (standard Google Analytics retention). Session recordings via Clarity are retained for up to 30 days. If you contact us via email, we retain your communication for as long as necessary to resolve your inquiry.

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Our site is served over HTTPS encryption.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal data, we will take steps to delete it.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated revision date.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:
